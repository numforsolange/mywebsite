Survived by Google Search Engine?

PUBLISHED ON March 20, 2018

Google search has become the top engine for most researches. Nowadays, when you ask someone a question he/she can’t figure out the answer, the response usually follows “search on google”. A common slogan among students “if it’s not on google, it’s nowhere else”. The rapid increase in internet usage has become the push drive to this search engine. How appealing it is, when you find what you are looking for. However, you don’t always get exact results but something related makes it less stressful.

Really, as GOOGLE puts it, “I’m Feeling Lucky”. I couldn’t imagine this search engine not playing a major role in successfully completing my research thesis, coupled with ‘devoirs’ and other tasks. A platform that connects to the entire world, which links to various networks, where you can find if not all, but most information searched.

Do you know, you can search on google while offline and you get notified on your search results once connected to the internet? Among other search engines, given the high accessibility, high network connectivity, results achieved, I am feeling lucky to have google search engine, making life, tasks, activities, et cetera, easier and fun.
